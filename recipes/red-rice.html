Red Rice

Red rice is a one of my kids' favorite dishes. It is a Vietnamese dish usually served with a protein, such as steak,on the side.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups Jasmines rice (cooked and cooled)
  • 2 Tb tomato paste
  • 1 Tb chopped garlic
  • 1 Tb Maggi Seasoning sauce
  • 1 Tb fish sauce
  • 1 Tb sugar
  • 4 Tb butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat up pan to high heat and add butter and garlic
  2. Fry garlic for 30 seconds and then add tomato paste
  3. Fry mixture for 1 minutes and add Maggi sauce, fish sauce and sugar. Stir until mixed.
  4. Add the rice and mix and fry until uniform in color
  5. Serve as a side with your favorite protein
